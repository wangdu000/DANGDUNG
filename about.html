School Overview

Established: 2014 A.D. (Estd. 2014) in Kathmandu’s Ganeshthan area, affiliated with the National Examinations Board (NEB) and recognized by Nepal’s Ministry of Education.

Affiliation & Management: Founded by a group of experienced professors and academicians. The institution is overseen by a board of directors, with the Principal acting as CEO, supported by a dedicated academic-management team.

Academic Programs

Offers +2 level education (Grades XI–XII) in:

  • Science
  • Management
  • Law

Admission requires:

  • An entrance test
  • Parent-student interview
  • Consideration of SEE grades for final selection

Facilities & Learning Environment

Features modern infrastructure: A/C classrooms, multimedia labs, science and computer labs, a library, auditorium, conference hall, cafeteria, sports area, and transport facilities.

Classroom sizes: ~25 students per class, supervised by two homeroom teachers.

Digital learning tools, well-equipped labs, and practical learning through field visits, projects, and case studies are emphasized.

School Culture & Philosophy

Motto: “Leading to the Light”

Mission: Promoting intellectual, social, and personal growth. Encouraging creativity, critical thinking, ethical values, and leadership skills.

Strong emphasis on student-centered, transparent, and socially inclusive learning aided by merit- and need-based scholarships, and flexible fee installment plans.

Co-curricular & Enrichment Activities

Co-curricular activities include:

  • Debate, elocution, essay writing, painting, drama, project work, and field trips

Hosts events like the Uniglobe Bahas (debate competition) and interschool festivities such as Uniglobe Futsal Frenzy.

Why Choose Uniglobe SS?

  • Modern infrastructure and student-friendly learning spaces
  • Hands-on, practical approach to education
  • Balanced emphasis on academics, co-curricular, and personal development
  • Transparent admission and evaluation processes
  • Focus on future readiness and leadership development through NEB‑approved +2 streams
